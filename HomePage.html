Welcome to our community!

Our Church is made up of diverse Orthodox traditions, from Greece, Romania, Serbia, Lebanon, Palestine, Russia, and the Ukraine. This unique event gives our neighbors in the community a chance to experience the tastes and sounds, dance, and religion.  We invite you visit our festival, enjoy the food, the music, and the culture.

  • Great Food, Beer & Wine
  • Live Music with Jesse Manno and friends
  • Ethnic Dancing
  • Church Tours
  • Byzantine Choir Singing
  • Books, Icons & Gifts
  • Free Parking & Admission
  • Jumping Castles and face painting!
